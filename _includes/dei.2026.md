While TBD strives to be a welcoming space, we recognize we aren't yet fully representative of all people. We believe our community has the agency to shape a more inclusive culture by being intentional about how we retreat.

This year, we are continuing our commitment to diversity by holding 10 of our 50 tickets specifically for BIPOC attendees. These spots will be held until July 23, after which any remaining tickets will be released to the general waitlist.

We ask our alumni to keep this initiative in mind when inviting others into the space. Together, we can build a retreat that is more representative and welcoming to everyone.
